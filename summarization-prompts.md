# Summarization Prompts

## 1. Executive Summary Prompt

```text
Summarize the following content for a busy professional.

Content: [PASTE CONTENT]

Output format:
- 5-line executive summary
- Key insights
- Risks or concerns
- Action points
- One-line conclusion

Keep the summary clear, accurate, and concise.
```

## 2. Research Paper Summary Prompt

```text
Summarize this research paper in simple English.

Paper text/abstract: [PASTE TEXT]

Include:
- Research objective
- Methodology
- Key findings
- Limitations
- Practical implications
- Simple explanation for non-technical readers
```
