# AI Content Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to assess AI-generated content.

| Criteria | Excellent | Good | Needs Improvement | Poor |
|---------|-----------|------|-------------------|------|
| Instruction Following | Fully follows all instructions | Misses minor details | Misses key requirements | Does not follow task |
| Accuracy | Factually reliable | Mostly accurate | Some inaccuracies | Major inaccuracies |
| Completeness | Covers all required points | Covers most points | Missing important points | Incomplete |
| Clarity | Clear and easy to read | Mostly clear | Some confusing areas | Difficult to understand |
| Tone | Matches requested tone | Slight mismatch | Noticeable mismatch | Wrong tone |
| Structure | Well organized | Mostly organized | Needs better flow | Poor structure |
| Usefulness | Highly useful | Useful | Limited usefulness | Not useful |

## Overall Rating

- Accept
- Needs Revision
- Reject
