# Case Study: Healthcare Content Prompt

## Objective
Create a clear and reader-friendly healthcare article using AI assistance.

## Prompt Used
```text
Act as a healthcare content writer. Write a simple article on preventive health checkups for working adults.

Audience: General readers
Tone: Clear, helpful, and trustworthy
Length: 800 words

Include:
- Why preventive checkups matter
- Common tests
- Recommended frequency
- Lifestyle tips
- Disclaimer to consult a doctor
```

## Evaluation Criteria
- Medical accuracy
- Simple language
- Clear structure
- Practical value
- Appropriate disclaimer

## Result
The prompt produced a structured article with useful sections, but required manual review for medical accuracy and tone refinement.

## Improvement
Added instruction: "Avoid making diagnosis or treatment claims. Keep advice general and encourage professional consultation."
