# Case Study: AI Output Evaluation

## Objective
Evaluate whether an AI response follows a multi-step instruction.

## Task
The AI was asked to write a 150-word LinkedIn post with a professional tone, 3 bullet points, and a final question.

## Evaluation Findings

| Criteria | Observation |
|---------|-------------|
| Word count | Slightly exceeded limit |
| Tone | Professional and clear |
| Formatting | Included bullet points |
| CTA | Final question included |
| Improvement Needed | Reduce length and improve opening hook |

## Final Decision
Needs minor revision.

## Revised Recommendation
Shorten the post to meet word count and make the first line more engaging.
