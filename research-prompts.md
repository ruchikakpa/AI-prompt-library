# Research Prompts

## 1. Topic Research Prompt

```text
Act as a research assistant. Research the topic: [TOPIC].

Provide:
- Short overview
- Key facts
- Current trends
- Important challenges
- Opportunities
- Practical applications
- Suggested sources to verify

Keep the language clear and suitable for a beginner-to-intermediate audience.
```

## 2. Competitor Content Analysis Prompt

```text
Analyze the following competitor content: [PASTE CONTENT OR SUMMARY].

Evaluate:
- Main topic angle
- Target audience
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- SEO opportunities
- Missing sections
- Better title suggestions
- Content improvement ideas
```
